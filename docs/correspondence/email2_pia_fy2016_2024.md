Dear City of Dallas Open Records Office,

I am submitting this public information request pursuant to the Texas Public Information Act, Tex. Gov't Code § 552.001 et seq.

**Records Requested**

I respectfully request the following records for fiscal years 2016 through 2024 (the City of Dallas fiscal year runs October 1 – September 30):

1. Actual expenditures (amounts paid/disbursed) per Capital Improvement Program (CIP) project, broken down by project number or project ID, for all bond programs active during FY2016–FY2024.
2. If available in your financial system, the council district(s) associated with each project at the time of expenditure.

**Scope clarification:** The City's Open Data Portal (Vendor Payments for Fiscal Year 2019–Present, dataset x5ih-idh7) currently reflects only FY2025–FY2026 transactions. Accordingly, this request covers the gap period of FY2016–FY2024.

Electronic format (CSV, Excel, or structured text export from your financial system) is strongly preferred. If records must be provided in PDF or paper format, please notify me before production so we may discuss alternatives.
